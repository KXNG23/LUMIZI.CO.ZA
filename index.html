A brief description LUMIZI PTY LTD Construction

Lumizi is a construction company based in Gauteng, located at 595 Dalmatian Drive, Garsfontein X10, Pretoria, Gauteng, 0042.
Established in 2015, the company specializes in delivering high-quality construction services, offering innovative solutions to meet the needs of residential, commercial, and industrial projects.
With a commitment to excellence and client satisfaction, Lumizi has built a strong reputation in the region.
